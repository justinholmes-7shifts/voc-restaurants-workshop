# VOC Restaurants Workshop

This repository is for Voice of Customer (VOC) analysis in the restaurant domain.

## Purpose

Voice of Customer analysis helps understand customer feedback, preferences, and pain points to improve restaurant services and experiences. This workshop provides tools and methodologies for collecting, analyzing, and acting on customer insights.

## What is Voice of Customer?

Voice of Customer (VOC) is a research method that captures customers' expectations, preferences, and aversions. In the restaurant industry, this includes:

- Customer feedback and reviews
- Dining experience insights
- Menu preferences and suggestions
- Service quality assessments
- Ambiance and atmosphere feedback

## Getting Started

This workshop will guide you through various VOC analysis techniques tailored for the restaurant industry.
